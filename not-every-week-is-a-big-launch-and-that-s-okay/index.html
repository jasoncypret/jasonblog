Not Every Week Is a Big Launch (And That's Okay)

A candid look at the unglamorous side of solo entrepreneurship, from wrestling with AWS configurations to shipping incremental BoxBoard improvements, and the lessons learned about estimation, frustration management, and celebrating small wins.
June 30, 2025

In startup land, there’s a tendency to glorify the big launch, the shiny new feature, the growth spike, the viral moment. But the truth? Most weeks don’t look like that. Some weeks are just plain messy. And that’s when the real work happens.
